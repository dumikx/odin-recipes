Musakka

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 eggplants
  • salt to taste
  • Â¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef

Steps

  1. Lay eggplant slices on paper towels; sprinkle lightly with salt. Let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture, then pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Warm olive oil in a skillet over high heat.
  3. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add parsley, fines herbs, cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg.
